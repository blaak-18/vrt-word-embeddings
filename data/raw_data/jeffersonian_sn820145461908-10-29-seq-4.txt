WHERE YOU CAN GET
ELECTORAL TICKETS.
Believing that the officers of the
State of Georgia were fair and hon
orable men, we have sent to the
Ordinary of each county 500 tickets,
requesting him to send some of them
to every precinct in his county. In
many instances, replies have already
been received to the effect that this
would willingly be done. We did not
request the courtesy of a reply at all
and so to receive these generous
promises to do us this kindness is
deeply appreciated. In all cases, we
believe the Ordinaries will take this
slight trouble to insure that each
citizen may have an opportunity to
vote his convictions, and we hope no
precinct will be without plenty of
tickets on election day.
Os course, we have sent tickets
into every county in ample quantities,
placing them in the hands of men on
whom we rely to help distribute them
effectively. They would be glad to
have you ask them for tickets. We
cannot print the full list of workers
but we give below several names in
